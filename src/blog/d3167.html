► IT Applications (D316/D317) I.E Comptia A+


Hey all, todays write up is for D316/D317 or better known as the Comptia A+ exam.


First I will go over the A+ exam objectives and then the study methods I used to pass.

The Comptia A+ is split up between two exams. The core 1 and the core 2 both exams have different exam objectives. I.E Core 1 is hardware focused. Core 2 is software focused.


Core 1

Domain Percentage of Exam
1.0 Mobile Devices. 15%
2.0 Networking. 20%
3.0 Hardware. 25%
4.0 Virtualization and Cloud Computing. 11%
5.0 Hardware and Network Troubleshooting. 29%
Full Core 1 Exam Objectives

Core 2

Domain Percentage of Exam
1.0 Operating Systems. 15%
2.0 Security. 20%
3.0 Software Troubleshooting. 25%
4.0 Operational Procedures. 11%
Full Core 2 Exam Objectives

Here is exactly what I did. (Process is the same for both exams)

  1. I recomend purchasing the course notes from Professor Messers website

  2. Watch the Professor Messer A+ 1101 and 1102 course videos.

  3. Listen to the Study Group playlists for 1101 and 1102. These are very beneficial for testing your retention.

  4. Jason Dions practice exams on udemy are free with your WGU student account. Core 1 Practice Exams , Practice Exams 1102 Take the first test immediately after finishing the course videos. This will tell you where your weak areas are, study those and then take test 2

  5. Rinse and repeat Step 4 until you are seeing passing scores on Practice Test 5, and then schedule your exam.

  6. Continue listening to the Study Groups until it is test day. If you like quizlet I recommend this 1101 Quizlet and 1102 Quizlet

This is what worked for me, you may need more or less. Don't be afraid to deviate from this.

If you don't pass your first attempt be sure to evaluate your weak areas and start again from Step 3

Good luck!
